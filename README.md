A chaotic prehistoric survival beat’em up made in **72 hours** for the **Mini Jam 211: Dinosaurs** game jam.

Fight waves of enemies, evolve through multiple dinosaurs, and survive long enough to become the ultimate predator.

---

## Gameplay

You start as a small dinosaur and grow stronger every few levels by transforming into a new species.

Each evolution changes:
- your appearance
- your animations
- your combat feel
- your power

Progress through increasingly dangerous waves until you finally become the mighty T-Rex.

---

## Features

- Multiple playable dinosaurs
- Evolution system tied to player level
- Fast-paced melee combat
- Enemy knockback and hit feedback
- Dynamic enemy scaling
- Pixel-art visuals
- Original game feel
- Made entirely in 72 hours

---

## Dinosaurs

Playable evolutions include:
- Raptor
- Dilophosaurus
- Gallimimus
- Triceratops
- Stegosaurus
- Crocodile
- T-Rex

---

## Controls

| Action | Input |
|---|---|
| Move | WASD |
| Attack | Left Click |
| Heavy Attack | E |

---

## Tech

Made with Godot Engine.

Main systems include:
- Resource-based character system
- AnimatedSprite2D workflow
- Area2D combat hitboxes
- Dynamic enemy scaling
- Camera smoothing
- Audio feedback system
- TileMap world building
